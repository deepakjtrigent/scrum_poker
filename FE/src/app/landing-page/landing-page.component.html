Scrum Poker

Scrum Poker For Agile Development Teams

Have fun while being productive with our simple and complete tool.

How it Works?

Step 1: Create or Join a Session

To start a new estimation session, click on the Create Session button. Alternatively, to join an existing session, ask the session creator for the unique session code and enter it in the "Join Session" field on the dashboard.

Step 2: Cast Your Vote

Once you have joined a session, you will be presented with the estimation cards (e.g., Fibonacci sequence) on your screen. Carefully review the task or user story being estimated. Choose the estimation card that best represents your assessment of the complexity of the task. Click on the card to cast your vote. Your vote will be anonymous and visible only to the facilitator.

Step 3: Wait for Votes

Wait for all team members to cast their votes. You will see a real-time update on the dashboard as team members vote.

Step 4: Reveal Votes

When everyone has voted, the session creator or facilitator can click on the Reveal Votes button to simultaneously reveal all the votes. The voting results will be displayed on the screen, allowing everyone to see the different estimates provided by team members.

Step 5: Discuss and Re-Vote (Optional)

If there are significant discrepancies in the estimates or if the team needs further clarification, use the "Discussion Board" to communicate and discuss the reasoning behind your estimates. If necessary, the team can re-vote on the task after the discussion to reach a consensus.

Step 6: End the Session

Once the team has reached a consensus or agreed on an estimate, the session creator can click on the End Session button to conclude the estimation process. The final estimate will be recorded and saved for future reference.
