Team estimations made easy

Simple Planning Poker

How it Works?

Step 1: Create or Join a Session

To start a new estimation session, click on the Start a Poker button. Alternatively, to join an existing session, ask the session creator for the URL and enter it in the "Enter Room" field on the dashboard.

Step 2: Cast Your Vote

Once you have joined a session, you will be presented with the estimation cards (e.g., Fibonacci sequence) on your screen. Carefully review the task or user story being estimated. Choose the estimation card that best represents your assessment of the complexity of the task. Click on the card to cast your vote. Your vote will remain anonymous until the facilitator clicks on the Reveal button, and after that, it will be visible to everyone.

Step 3: Wait for Votes

Wait for all team members to cast their votes. You will see a real-time update on the dashboard as team members vote.

Step 4: Reveal Votes

When everyone has voted, the session creator or facilitator can click on the Reveal Votes button to simultaneously reveal all the votes. The voting results will be displayed on the screen, allowing everyone to see the different estimates provided by team members.

Step 5: Reset Votes

After completing the story point assignment, the "Reset Votes" button allows facilitators to reset the story points, facilitating the process for further story point assignments.

Step 6: End the Session

After the team has collectively reached a consensus or unanimously agreed upon an estimate, participants are free to exit from the Planning Poker
